To get Started!

  • Click on START GAME
  • Fill in your name
  • Choose your team
  • Time to Play
  • Have Fun!!!!.

Rules:

  1. Click GO to roll the dice
  2. Everytime you click on the dice will pop up a hints for Computer Science knowledges
  3. If the land is not occupied you can choose whether to occupy the land or decline
  4. If the land was occupied, you need to give up 1/4 of the rent for staying at the land
  5. If you jump on a CHALLENGE, you need to choose difficulty for the challenge. (Price are different according to the difficulty)
  6. Lucky 1 make the land fall into disuse
  7. Lucky 2 Randomly occupy a land
  8. Lucky 3 Get you back to the Starting Point
